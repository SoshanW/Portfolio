About Soshan Wijayarathne

Hi, I'm Soshan Wijayarathne, a computer science undergraduate at the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT), Sri Lanka, affiliated with the University of Westminster. I'm passionate about technology, software development, and building solutions that solve real-world problems. I’m constantly exploring new ideas, especially in full-stack development and interactive technologies.

My journey began with a fascination for how technology can transform lives. I’ve honed my skills in front-end and back-end development, and I've worked on numerous projects, ranging from websites to complex applications that tackle unique challenges in society.

Technical Skills

  • Frameworks & Libraries: React, Node.js, Express.js, Bootstrap
  • Database: MySQL, MongoDB
  • Version Control: Git, GitHub
  • Tools: Visual Studio Code, IntelliJ IDEA, Figma

Music & Creative Work

In addition to my tech skills, music has always been a big part of my life. I'm a musician who sings, plays piano, and guitar. I’ve led and taught choirs, including the well-recognized choir, 'Acoustics'. I enjoy blending my creative passions with technology, working on projects that bring music and tech together in innovative ways.
